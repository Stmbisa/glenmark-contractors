About Glenmark Contractors

GLENMARK CONTRACTORS LTD was incorporated in 2021 as an indigenous Ugandan firm with a mission to provide efficient and effective professional services, supplies, civil & building construction, and general contracts.

We offer relief and non-relief general supplies and merchandise to companies and organizations with a proven track record. Our clients are our partners, and over time we have made them profitable, convenient, and made life easier for them.

With over 100 employees during full-time engagement periods, we pride ourselves on our structured business approach and quality control procedures that meet customer expectations.
